The Technology Management Club (TMC) at Indiana University is the Kelley School of Business’s leading organization for students passionate about technology and its role in shaping modern business. Our mission is to explore emerging tech trends, cultivate leadership skills, and help members grow professionally in tech-driven industries.

We achieve this through a diverse range of activities including:

• Guest speaker events featuring industry professionals

• Networking sessions with peers, alumni, and employers

• Interactive workshops and learning seminars

• Community service projects and outreach

• Leadership development opportunities

• Tech-focused social events

TMC is open to students of all majors and experience levels who are curious about the intersection of business and technology. Our membership continues to grow each semester as we build a supportive, innovative, and collaborative community.
