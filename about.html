About TMC

Shaping the Future of Business Through Technology

We are Indiana University's leading student organization at the intersection of technology and business, empowering the next generation of tech leaders.

Our Mission

Explore. Connect. Lead.

The Technology Management Club at Indiana University is the Kelley School of Business's leading organization for students passionate about technology and its role in shaping modern business.

Our mission is to explore emerging tech trends, cultivate leadership skills, and help members grow professionally in tech-driven industries.

TMC is open to students of all majors and experience levels who are curious about the intersection of business and technology. Our membership continues to grow each semester as we build a supportive, innovative, and collaborative community.

Our Values

What Drives Us

Innovation

We stay at the forefront of emerging technology trends and encourage creative thinking across all our initiatives.

Community

We build a supportive, inclusive environment where students of all backgrounds can collaborate, learn, and grow together.

Professional Growth

We provide hands-on opportunities to develop leadership skills, technical knowledge, and industry connections that accelerate careers.

What We Do

Our Activities

TMC hosts a diverse range of activities designed to build skills, expand networks, and create lasting connections.

Guest Speaker Events

Industry professionals share insights on technology strategy, consulting, product management, and career development.

Networking Sessions

Curated opportunities to connect with peers, alumni, and employers from leading technology and consulting firms.

Interactive Workshops

Hands-on learning seminars covering everything from coding fundamentals to business analytics and consulting frameworks.

Community Service & Social Events

Community outreach projects and social gatherings that build connections beyond the classroom and give back to the community.
